Translate & answer the questions:
1. Your parents come back to Wroclaw tomorrow?

4. anata wa ima ie ni kaerimasuka?
5. kurisumasu ni anata wa kazoku no ie ni kaerimasuka?
6. itsu saru wa doubutsuen ni kaerimasuka?
