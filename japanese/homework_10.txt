Homework A1 60a
Last updated: 09.11.2020

1. PWr is where in Poland?
2. Museum is where in Wrocław?
3. Your bag is where in the house?
4. Microwave is in your room?
5. Bathtub is in the toilet?
6. Chimney is where?
7. Post office is where in the city?
8. Your home is on Rynek in Wrocław?

1. pwr wa po-rando no doko ni arimasuka?
   pwr wa po-rando no wroclaw ni arimasu
2. hakubutsukan wa wroclaw no doko ni arimasuka?
   hakubutsukan wa wroclaw no pl. grunwaldzki ni arimasu
3. anata no kaban wa eki no doko ni arimasuka?
   watashi no kaban wa eki no yuka ni arimasu
4. denshi renji wa anata no heya ni arimasuka?
   iie, denshi renji wa kitchen ni arimasu
5. ofuro wa toiru ni arimasuka?
   iie, ofuro wa furoba ni arimasu
6. entotsu wa doko ni arimasuka?
   entotsu wa yane ni arimasu
7. yuubinkyoku wa machi no doko ni arimasuka?
   yuubinkyoku wa machi no pl. grunwaldzki ni arimasu
8. anata no ie wa wroclaw no rynku ni arimasuka?
   watashi no ie wa wroclaw no t4 ni arimasu
