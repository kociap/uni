Please translate the questions & answer them in Japanese
1. Your country is where? 
2. His school is where?
3. Post office is here?
4. Windows are where?
5. Museum is over there?*
6. Your shoes are where?
7. That is a police station?
8. She is a person from which bank?
9. My bag is where?

* use ASOKO

1. anata no kuni wa doko desuka?
2. kare no gakku wa doko desuka?
3. yuubinkyoku wa koko desuka?
4. doko wa mado desuka?
5. hakubutsukan wa asoko desuka?
6. anata no kutsu wa doko desuka?
7. soko wa keisatsusho desuka?
8. kanojo wa dochira no ginkou no kata desuka?
9. watashi no kaban wa doko desuka?


vocabulary:
here = koko ここ
there = soko そこ
over there = asoko あそこ
***
village = mura 村
post office = yuubinkyoku　郵便局
police station = keisatsusho　警察署
church = kyoukai　教会
park = kouen　公園
bank = ginkou　銀行
theatre = gekijou　劇場
cinema = eigakan　映画館
museum = hakubutsukan　博物館
library = toshokan　図書館
