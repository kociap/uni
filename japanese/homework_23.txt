///////////////////////////////
Translate & then answer the questions
1. After this, you clean your room?
kore kara anata wa anata no heya no souji o shimasuka?
2. Now you study what?
ima anata wa nan no benkyou o shimasuka?
3. You drive a car? or a motorcycle?
anata wa kuruma no unten o shimasuka? soretomo, baiku no unten o shimasuka?
4. From when, your family has a meal?
itsu kara anata no kazoku wa shokuji o shimasuka?
5. You study this evening, until morning?
kyou no yoru ashita no asa made anata wa benkyou o shimasuka?
6. You do what homework?
anata wa nan no shukudai o shimasuka?
7. Your father will take what (animal) for a walk, after this?
anata no chichi wa kore kara nan no sanpo o shimasuka?
8. You study Japanese this afternoon? Or you play baseball?
kyou no hiru anata wa nihongo no benkyou o shimasuka? soretomo, basketto bo-ru o shimasuka?
9. After that, you do laundry with this washing machine?
sore kara anata wa sentakuki de sentaku o shimasuka?
10. This morning, you go for a walk?
kyou no asa anata wa sanpo o shimasuka?
11. Your friend plays what sports?
anata no tomodachi wa nan no supo-tsu o shimasuka?
12. When you clean the kitchen with vacuum cleaner?
itsu anata wa kitchen no soujiki no souji o shimasuka?
13. Today you do what?
kyou anata wa nani o shimasuka?
14. You do homework now?
ima anata wa shukudai o shimasuka?
15. Until when, you take a break?
itsu made anata wa kyuukei o shimasuka?

Fill the missing connectors & endings to complete the sentence.

16. From morning their cousin cooks today.
asa [1] kanojora [2] itoko [3] kyou [4] ryouri [5] simas[6].
asa kara kanojora no itoko wa kyou ryouri o shimasu

17. On this PC, he studies English.
kono [1] pc [2] kare [3] eigo [4] benkyou [5] simas[6].
kono pc kare wa eigo no benkyou o shimasu

18. After that, her friend doesn't go to Warsaw by car.
sore [1] kanojo [2] tomodachi [3] kuruma [4] warushawa [5] ikimasen[6].
sore kara kanojo no tomodachi wa kuruma de watushawa niwa ikimasen

19. When tomorrow you do Christmas shopping?
ashita [1] itsu [2] anata [3] kurisumasu [4] kaimono [5] simas[6]?
ashita no itsu anata wa kurisumasu no kaimono o shimasuka?

20. My friends take this dog for walk until evening.
watashi [1] tomodachi [2] kono [3] inu [4] sanpo [5] yoru [6] simas[7].
watashi no tomodachi wa kono inu no sanpo o yoru made shimasu
