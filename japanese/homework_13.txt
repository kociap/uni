Translate and then answer the questions:
1. TV is in front of what?
2. Magazines are under the telephone?
3. Telephone is in front of what?
4. Oven is on the right to the refrigerator?

1. TV wa nan no mae ni arimasuka?
   hai, TV wa tsukue no mae ni arimasu
2. zasshi wa denwa no shita ni arimasuka?
   iie, zasshi wa denwa no hidari ni arimasu
3. denwa wa nan no mae ni arimasuka?
   hai, denwa wa reizouko no mae ni arimasu
4. o-bun wa reizouko no migi ni arimasuka?
   hai, o-bun wa reizouko no migi ni arimasu

Answer the questions:
5. Fuku wa Nan no Hidari ni arimaska?
6. Yakan wa Doko ni arimaska?
7. Denwa wa Inu no Doko ni arimaska?
8. Zasshi wa Doko ni arimaska?
9. Tokei wa Neko no Doko ni arimaska?
10. Reizouko wa Doko ni arimaska?

6. yakan wa o-bun no ue ni arimasu
