Homework A1 60a
Last updated: 16.11.2020

1. My PC is in front of me.
2. Refrigerator is next to the sink in the kitchen.
3. Your shoes are under the bed?
4. Germany is on the right from Poland?
5. Your books are on (the surface of) the desk in your room?
6. Living room is behind the door?
7. Her car is in front of the entrance?
8. Garden is behind the house?
9. France is on the left of which country?

1. watashi no pc wa watashi no mae ni arimasu
2. reizouko wa kitchen no nagashi no yoko ni arimasu
3. anata no kutsu wa beddo no shita ni arimasuka?
4. doitsu wa po-rando no migi ni arimasuka?
5. anata no hon wa anata no heya no tsukue no ue ni arimasuka?
6. living room wa doa no ushro ni arimasuka?
7. anata no kuruma wa iriguchi no mae ni arimasuka?
8. niwa wa ie no ushiro ni arimasuka?
9. furansu wa dochira no kuni no hidari ni arimasuka?

vocabulary:
front = mae 前
back/behind = ushiro 後ろ

monkey = saru (uki uki u kiki) gorira (uho uho u hoho)
