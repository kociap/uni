Homework A1 60a
Last updated: 25.11.2020

1. Lion is in that zoo?
2. Your friend is in this university?
3. My father's chickens are in the garden?
4. Your younger sister is in China?
5. Their mother is at home? Or, at a railway station? 
6. Elephant is in the zoo? Or, in a park?
7. Pigs are where in that house?
8. My rabbit is outside the house?
9. This horse is whose horse?

1. raion wa sono doubutsuen ni imasuka?
2. anata no tomodachi wa kono kouka daigaku ni imasuka?
3. watashi no chichi no niwatori wa niwa ni imasuka?
4. anata no ani wa chuugoku ni imasuka?
5. karera no haha wa ie ni imasuka? soretomo, eki ni imasuka?
6. zou wa doubutsuen ni imasuka? soretomo, kouen ni imasuka?
7. buta wa sono ie no doko ni imasuka?
8. watashi no usagi wa ie no soto ni imasuka?
9. kono uma wa dare no uma desuka?

vocabulary:
this X = kono X
that X = sono X, or ano X
***
family = kazoku 家族
father = chichi 父
(dad = otousan お父さん)
mother = haha 母
(mama= okaasan お母さん)
older sister = ane 姉
older brother = ani 兄
younger sister = imouto 妹
younger brother = otouto 弟
friend(s) = tomodachi 友達
