Homework A1 60a
Last updated:13.11.2020

1. Towel is where in the house?
2. TV is where in the house?
3. Toilet is above the kitchen?
4. Living room is under your room?

1. taoru wa ie no doko ni arimasuka?
   taoru wa furoba ni arimasu.
2. terebi wa ie no doko ni arimasuka?
   terebi wa living room ni arimasu.
3. toiru wa kitchen no ue ni arimasuka?
   iie, toiru wa kitchen no shita ni arimasu.
4. living room wa anata no heya no shita ni arimasuka?
   hai, living room wa watashi no heya no shita ni arimasu.

Use the picture from the lesson (https://www.dropbox.com/s/0wc6c24es9a8gxj/homework-pic01.png)
and answer the questions:
5. Kitchen wa Living room no Doko ni arimaska?
6. Toire wa Furoba no Doko ni arimaska?
7. Furoba wa Toire no Doko ni arimaska?
8. Living room wa Watashi no heya no Doko no Kitchen no Doko ni arimaska?

5. kitchen wa living room no hidari ni arimasu
6. toire wa furoba no hidari ni arimasu
7. furowa wa toire no migi ni arimasu
8. living room wa anata no heya no shita no kitchen no migi ni arimasu

vocabulary:
→ = migi 右
← = hidari 左
↑= ue 上
↓= shita 下
